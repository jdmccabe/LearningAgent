# System Architecture Description

Document: ATGSD-SAD-001
Revision: C

## Major Elements

- Structural base frame made from welded carbon-steel rectangular tube.
- Removable ASME-certified accumulator assembly.
- Nitrogen and oxygen-compatible process piping manifold.
- Electrical ground support equipment cabinet with 120 VAC service, 24 VDC control power, emergency stop chain, and programmable interlock relay.
- Flight-hardware-facing harnesses terminated in simulator umbilical connectors.
- Passive sheet-metal panels, lifting lugs, and access platforms.

## Exclusions

The architecture excludes explosive, pyrotechnic, and energetic devices. No ordnance storage, firing circuit, initiator, safe-and-arm device, or pyrotechnic connector exists in the design.

The architecture excludes radio-frequency communication. The system has no wireless communication hardware, no wireless telemetry, and no remote-control receiver.

The architecture excludes flight pressure vessels other than the removable certified accumulator. Pressure boundaries are ground support equipment only.

## Interfaces

External interfaces are documented in ATGSD-ICD-010 Rev B:

- J1/J2 flight-hardware simulator electrical umbilicals.
- GN2 supply quick-disconnect QD-GN2-01.
- GOX service simulator quick-disconnect QD-OX-01.
- Facility power disconnect DS-1.
- Lift fixture attach points LF-1 through LF-4.

Configuration drawings ATGSD-DWG-1000 through ATGSD-DWG-1075 identify externally visible interfaces, grounding studs, lifting points, pressure ports, and maintenance access zones.
