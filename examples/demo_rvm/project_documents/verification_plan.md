# Verification Plan

Document: ATGSD-VP-001
Revision: C

## Planned Procedures

- ATP-RS-010: Range safety and launch-site operations hazard control inspection.
- ATP-STR-020: Structural analysis review, proof-load inspection, and weld/NDE record review.
- ATP-ELEC-030: Electrical code inspection, bonding-resistance test, emergency-stop demonstration, and interlock functional test.
- ATP-WIRE-040: Harness workmanship inspection and continuity/insulation-resistance test.
- ATP-MAT-050: Materials compatibility and flammability-screening record review.
- ATP-OXY-060: Oxygen compatibility, cleanliness, and assembly-control inspection.
- ATP-PRES-070: Pressure-vessel certificate review, B31.3 hydrostatic test, and pneumatic leak check.
- ATP-GSE-080: Ground support equipment drawing release, labeling, access, lifting, and maintainability inspection.
- ATP-NDE-090: NDE personnel qualification and certification record review.
- ATP-PROC-100: Heat-treatment, coating, plating, and corrosion-protection record review.
- ATP-PACK-120: Packaging, preservation, transport, and FOD-control inspection.

## Objective Acceptance Examples

Bonding resistance shall be less than or equal to 2.5 milliohms from each bonded item to the equipment grounding bar.

Pressure-decay during pneumatic leak check shall be less than or equal to 1 psig over 10 minutes at maximum operating pressure.

Emergency stop actuation shall remove control power from all simulated flow-enable outputs within 250 milliseconds.

Hydrostatic test pressure shall be 1.5 times design pressure with no visible leakage, permanent deformation, or pressure loss outside instrument tolerance.

Hot-dip galvanized coating thickness shall be greater than or equal to 85 micrometers average thickness with no bare areas.

Harness insulation resistance shall be greater than or equal to 100 megaohms at 500 VDC.
