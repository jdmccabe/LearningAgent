# Electrical Design and Workmanship Plan

Document: ATGSD-EL-PLAN-004
Revision: B

Facility power enters at disconnect DS-1 and is protected by branch-circuit breaker CB-1. Panel labels, arc-flash labels, lockout points, and maintenance clearances are identified on released drawing ATGSD-DWG-2200.

Bonding and grounding use a single-point equipment grounding bar, braided bonding jumpers across removable panels, and resistance checks at each external interface. The acceptance limit is less than or equal to 2.5 milliohms from each bonded item to the equipment grounding bar.

The electrical cabinet contains a hardwired emergency stop chain and an interlock relay that prevents simulated oxygen flow unless door closed, ground verified, pressure relief installed, and purge complete signals are present.

Harnesses H-100 through H-140 use qualified insulated wire procured to MIL-DTL-16878 slash-sheet requirements. Crimping, soldering, wire marking, strain relief, and inspection are controlled by ATGSD-WP-8739-004, which tailors NASA-STD-8739.4 workmanship checkpoints.

The project has no wireless communication hardware and no radio-frequency transmitters.
