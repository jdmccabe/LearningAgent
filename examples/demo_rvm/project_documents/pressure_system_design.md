# Pressure System Design Report

Document: ATGSD-PSDR-070
Revision: A

The pressure system consists of a removable 600 psig accumulator, a 450 psig GN2/GOX process piping manifold, flexible jumpers, relief valves, isolation valves, pressure transducers, and cleanliness-controlled quick disconnects.

The removable accumulator is purchased as an ASME BPVC Section VIII certified vessel. The receiving package includes manufacturer certificate of conformance, hydrostatic test report, relief-valve sizing sheet, material certification, and nameplate photograph.

The process piping manifold is designed to ASME B31.3 normal fluid service with project-specific oxygen-cleanliness controls. Verification includes calculation package ATGSD-CALC-073, drawing inspection, weld/NDE record review, 1.5 times design-pressure hydrostatic test, pneumatic leak check at maximum operating pressure, and pressure-decay acceptance less than or equal to 1 psig over 10 minutes.

Flexible hoses are shelf-life controlled and must have proof-pressure certification within the prior 12 months before use. Cleanliness caps remain installed until final connection.

Oxygen-service components are cleaned, bagged, and tagged by ATGSD-CLEAN-ISO14952-001. Lubricants and seals are restricted to oxygen-compatible materials on the approved parts list.
